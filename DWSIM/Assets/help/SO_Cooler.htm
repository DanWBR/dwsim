Cooler

The cooler simulates a stream cooling process.

Input Parameters

Pressure drop: pressure drop in the cooler.
Efficiency: cooler efficiency.
Calculation mode: depending on calculation mode a dedicated parameter is specified:

Mode Parameter
HeatRemoved: amount of heat removed by the cooler
OutletTemperature: Outlet temperature
OutletVapourFraction: Molar fraction of vapour phase

Calculation

The outlet stream temperature is calculated by doing a PH Flash, were the outlet stream enthalpy is calculated by a energy balance in the cooler.

Output Parameters

Delta-T: Temperature drop observed in the cooling process.
Heat removed: Heat removed from the process.
